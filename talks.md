# Talks & Presentations

## Recent NLP & Low-Resource Language Talks

### 2026
**Low-Resource Languages as Stress Tests for NLP Data**  
Berlin Buzzwords 2026  
Berlin, Germany

Talk examining how low-resource languages expose hidden weaknesses in NLP systems, particularly in evaluation design, annotation practices, and model robustness.

---

### 2026
**A Morpho-Syntactically Annotated Corpus of Ògè Folk Narratives with a Focus on Nominal Structure**  
RAIL Workshop @ LREC-COLING 2026  
Palma de Mallorca, Spain

Presentation on the development of a linguistically grounded annotated corpus for Ògè, focusing on nominal structure and interpretation in low-resource settings.

---

### 2026
**Modifier Ordering and Lexicogrammatical Structure in Ògè Nominal Phrases**  
LxGr2026 Online Symposium

Presentation examining modifier ordering patterns and lexicogrammatical structure in Ògè nominal phrases.
