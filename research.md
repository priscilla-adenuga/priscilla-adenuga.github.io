# Research

## 🧠 Research Focus

My work sits at the intersection of linguistics and Natural Language Processing (NLP), with a focus on how linguistic structure can inform computational modeling and evaluation.

## 🔍 Current Interests

- Syntax and its role in NLP models  
- Negation and meaning representation  
- Low-resource languages as stress tests for NLP  
- Linguistically-informed evaluation methods  

## 📄 Ongoing Work

- Morpho-syntactically annotated corpus of Ògè  
- Structural analysis of nominal modifiers  
- Evaluation of NLP systems on low-resource data
